LLM-Based Autonomous Driving Systems
We developed a LLM-based autonomous driving system that mitigates hallucinations in scene/object understanding, improves robustness on long-tail scenarios, and fully leverages complementary modality strengths (camera, LiDAR).
Domain Adaptation for LiDAR Semantic Segmentation
We developed the first lightweight LiDAR segmentation framework based on Hyperdimensional Computing (HDC) for adapting to streaming point cloud scans after deployment.

Underwater Video Enhancement
We developed a wavelet-based framework tailored to address temporal artifacts in underwater video enhancement, which often arise from the difficulty of acquiring paired underwater video data.
