LLM-Based Autonomous Driving Systems
We developed an LLM-based autonomous driving system that mitigates hallucinations in fine-grained factual and relational reasoning, while fully leveraging the complementary strengths of camera and LiDAR modalities.
Domain Adaptation for LiDAR Semantic Segmentation
We developed the first lightweight LiDAR segmentation framework based on Hyperdimensional Computing for adapting to streaming point cloud scans after deployment.
DAC 2026, under review.
Underwater Video Enhancement
We developed a wavelet-based framework tailored to address temporal artifacts in underwater video enhancement, which often arise from the difficulty of acquiring paired underwater video data.
IEEE TNNLS, under review.
